What is Flexbox?

Flexbox, or the Flexible Box Module, is a one-dimensional layout method for arranging items in rows or columns. It gives you more control over the alignment, order, and spacing of items within a container, even when their size is unknown or dynamic.
Flexbox is a powerful tool for modern web development, making it easier to build complex and flexible UIs with less code.
